Typing Practice Instructions

Here's how it works...

This web app helps you improve your typing skills.

Just start typing and aim for accuracy and speed.

Stats you'll see:

- Completed: The number of sentences you've completed.

- Mistakes: The number of mistakes you've made during your session.

- Speed: The completion time of your last sentence.

- Best Time: Your fastest completion time during this session.

- Accuracy: Accuracy of your last sentance.

Enjoy practicing and enhancing your typing skills! :)
